La Machina

Hi everyone!!! so i started working on this portable console project using the following:

-Intel Compute Stick (M3)
-Aliexpress 5 inch screen
-Aliexpress Phone controller
-Amazon Power bank
-2x Micro USB cables
-some cardboard and glue

I started with taking off the plastic layer off the cables so I can fit them better (they will be thinner and more flexible)

Then I made a cardboard case where I glued everything in and yeah that's it (it took me a whole day XD)
